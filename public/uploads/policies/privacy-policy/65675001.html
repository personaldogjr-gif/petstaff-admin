Further information about the processing of Personal Data

    • iubenda as Data Controller

      iubenda processes, as Data Controller, the following access data which is strictly necessary for the technical provision of the service:

      * IP address;
      * Web browser info (User Agent/type of device);
      * Referrer (page viewed by the User) and all the information possibly available on the http header.

      The information outlined above is sent to Bunny CDN which, in turn, forwards it to Akamai CDN. This forwarding is done, in the case of users in the European Union, without transmitting their IP address.

      Further information about Akamai CDN and Bunny CDN can be found in the section "Detailed information on the processing of Personal Data" in the present document.
